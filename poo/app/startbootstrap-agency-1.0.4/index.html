Pool FAQ

What exactly is a pool?

We define the pool as a plan to get a gift that you would like to carry out with your friends/colleagues'. Our mission is to enable the same at one place where you and your friends can discuss, chat together as well as pay your own shares for the same. Then, you can redeem the complete amount quickly instead of going through bank transfers, cash handling or the uncomfortable 'You Owe Me's.

Do the group members have to be in my mail list?

No, if a person is not in your Gmail or other mail list, you can feed in his basic details such as Name and E-Mail address directly. In this case, each of them would get the link to 'Confirm' in their inbox via Poolclues. However, you will notice that it becomes very convenient to invite via a single click if they are already in your Gmail contacts.

Is a facebook account required to set up my pool?

No, you can enjoy the whole process without using Facebook at all. However, in case you have a Facebook account, signing up (on the mobile) and adding friends to the pool becomes much more convenient and enjoyable!

Does setting up such a pool cost me extra money?

Not at all! Creating and completing a pool has no hidden charges at any time

Can I pool in money for any product? Can I add details like photo etc to share with the members?

Absolutely! You can utilize the target amount as you like as it belongs to you and your group members. For the details, you have the option for adding pool name, picture etc on the very top itself when you start creating the pool via poolclues

Can I decide the amount that each friend needs to pay differently?

Yes, we have that provision quite handy. Just select ‘Split unequally’ option above the list of friends in the process. After that, you can enter the amount desired in the fields differently but the total should remain equal to the target amount that you wish to collect.

Do contributors have to pay the amount I have allocated to them?

Yes, if you split the amount equally or allocate the money to be chipped in by them, they would have to pay the amount you allocate. The total amount gets adjusted accordingly whenever you add a person from the group.

Can I invite more people later or is this my only chance to invite people?

Absolutely! You can invite more friends at a later stage as well to contribute to the pool. More members means more fun and more sharing hands!
